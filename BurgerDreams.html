Burger Dreams

Description

This video game was created by Joan Barduena and me using Unity for the Artificial Intelligence subject.

The idea was to create a god game with an enviroment and diferent types of agents, each one of them with their own paths and routes to simulate the basics of the ecosystem of agents.

We used steering behaviours for the movement of the agents and behaviour trees to design their behaviours.
