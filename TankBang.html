TankBang

Description

An online multiplayer game made with Unity where the only winner is the last tank standing. Multiplayer was made using PUN2 library and it can be played up to 4 people.

TankBang is inspired by the famous Wii Play Tanks, having the same principal mechanic: moving, aiming and shooting. We added 5 different powerUps aswell that can be dropped when destroying wooden crates, those are bouncing bullets, health regen, speed boost, attack speed boost and mini tank ally.
