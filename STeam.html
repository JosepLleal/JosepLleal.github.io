S-Team

Description

Video game developed during the GranCITM Game Jam by the team MarteKings that lasted 34 hours.

S-Team is a 3D puzzle game where the player must swap between five woman, each one with huge knowledge on one field of STEAM (Sceince, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics).

We ended up winning the price for the game with best technology.
